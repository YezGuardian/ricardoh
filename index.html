About Ricardo Hansby

A Legacy of Strategic Leadership and Impact

Ricardo Hansby has a distinguished career built on a foundation of fostering collaboration and driving impactful economic change. For more than two decades, he has been at the forefront of economic development, from shaping municipal infrastructure policies to guiding renewable energy strategies across Southern Africa.

His professional journey is marked by a commitment to social and economic progress, beginning with his activism in the 1980s and continuing through high-level roles in government and the private sector. He possesses a unique ability to bridge the gap between public policy and private enterprise, creating solutions that are both innovative and practical.

Ricardo co-authored a significant research report on the impact of municipal dysfunction on SMMEs and managed the development of a national monitoring and evaluation system for the Department of Small Business Development. He is a respected voice in the energy sector, serving on the Energy Council of South Africa's Policy and Regulatory Standing Committee and the Industrialisation and Just Energy Transition (JET) Standing Committee.
